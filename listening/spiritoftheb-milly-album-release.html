SPIRIT OF THE BEEHIVE 1 and Milly 2 release, 'ENTERNTAINMENT, DEATH' and 'Wish Goes On'

April 9th, 2021

I really wanted this to be a working SPA React app with, what I was planning to use, the "Universal-Router" router instead of React's router React Router, because I could save some space, I guess. Aside from that, I decided to focus more on content than functionality; for now this will just be a static page dedicated to the release of 'ENTERNTAINMENT, DEATH' and 'Wish Goes On'.

Today, SPIRIT OF THE BEEHIVE and Milly release, an album 'ENTERNTAINMENT, DEATH' and EP 'Wish Goes On' respectively. 'ED' is 11 tracks long (3 being previously released singles; 2nd track "THERE'S NOTHING YOU CAN'T DO", 7th track "THE SERVER IS IMMERSED", and 10th track "I SUCK THE DEVIL'S COCK". 'WGO' is 5 tracks long (3 also being previously released singles; 2nd track "Denial", 3rd track "Star Thistle Blossom", and 5th track "Birds Fly Free". They're being grouped together out of convenience; both make alternative indie rock lo-fi more dream pop than shoegaze esque music, they will inevitably release music together, and I listen to both of them.

I haven't listened to either project in its entirety, excluding the previously released singles, but today I get the opportunity to listen to both the same day they are released. I will first listen to 'ED' in it's entirety, then 'WGO', and give my take aways for both projects.

I listened to both, they were amazing thank you.
